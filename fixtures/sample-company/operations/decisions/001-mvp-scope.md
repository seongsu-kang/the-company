# Decision 001: MVP Scope

> Status: Approved

**Date**: 2026-01-20
**Decided by**: CEO

## Decision

Focus MVP on three core features: AI task creation, auto-assignment, and progress tracking. Defer integrations (Slack, GitHub) to post-launch.

## Rationale

- Ship faster with smaller scope
- Validate core AI delegation before building integrations
- Integrations are "nice to have" not "must have" for early adopters
