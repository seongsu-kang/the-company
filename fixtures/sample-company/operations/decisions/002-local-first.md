# Decision 002: Local-First Architecture

> Status: Approved

**Date**: 2026-02-01
**Decided by**: CEO

## Decision

Adopt local-first architecture. All data stored as files, no mandatory cloud dependency. Users own their data.

## Rationale

- Privacy-conscious enterprise customers prefer on-prem
- Reduces infrastructure costs
- Enables offline usage
- Differentiator vs cloud-only competitors
