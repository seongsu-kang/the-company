# Task Management Market Analysis

> Overview of the task/project management landscape and Acme Corp's positioning

**Author**: CTO | **Date**: 2026-01-20 | **Status**: Active

---

## TL;DR

- Market size $4.5B (2025), growing 13% YoY
- Top players: Asana ($650M ARR), Monday.com ($800M ARR), Linear (private, est. $50M ARR)
- Gap: No tool offers AI that *does* tasks — only AI that *suggests* or *organizes*
- Opportunity: "AI team member" positioning vs "AI-enhanced tool"

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Tool | Strengths | Weakness | AI Features |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Linear** | Dev-focused, fast, beautiful | Limited to issue tracking | Auto-triage, suggestions |
| **Asana** | Enterprise, workflows | Heavy, slow | AI summaries |
| **Notion** | Flexible, docs+tasks | Too generic | AI writing only |
| **TaskFlow** | AI does work, not just organizes | Early stage | Full AI delegation |

## Key Trends

1. **AI-native tools** replacing bolt-on AI features
2. **Agent-based** workflows gaining traction (2025-2026)
3. **Privacy concerns** driving self-hosted/local-first demand
4. **Developer tools** market more willing to adopt AI early
